17th-century Anticipation of Newton and Leibniz

While the invention of calculus is usually attributed to Newton and Leibniz, a number of earlier figures, among them Pierre de Fermat, Rene Descartes, Blaise Pascal and others, laid the groundwork for Newton and Leibniz's development of the subject.
